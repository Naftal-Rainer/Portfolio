UBER - AMBULANCE NAIROBI PERAMBULATION CHALLENGE.

Given information on thousands of traffic accidents that occurred in Nairobi, Kenya in 2018 and 2019, as identified by the World Bank DIME research team and Flare, I set out to find locations for 6 ambulances every 3 hours during the test period. We will calculate the distance from each crash recorded in the training data to the nearest ambulance based on our scheduled locations. The total distance traveled (in decimal degrees) by all ambulances during the test period determines the final score. The goal is to place the six ambulances in locations that minimize the final score.
